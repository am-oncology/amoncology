Biography & Expertise

Professional Profile

Dr Asad Mahmood is a Consultant Clinical Oncologist at Barts Health NHS Trust.

He specialises in the use of radiotherapy and systemic therapies for the management of gastrointestinal and neurological cancers. He has a particular interest in advanced radiotherapy techniques including stereotactic radiosurgery/radiotherapy and contact brachytherapy.
